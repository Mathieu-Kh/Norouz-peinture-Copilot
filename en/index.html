About Mathieu Khalesi

I am Mathieu Khalesi, founder of Norouz Peinture. I have been working in France for over 16 years in the field of interior building painting.

I hold a CAP Painting diploma and have worked on numerous projects: apartments, houses, offices, shops, co‑ownerships and professional spaces.
