About Norouz Peinture

A company built on experience, quality and trust.

Mathieu Khalesi

I am Mathieu Khalesi, founder of Norouz Peinture. I have been working in France for more than 16 years in the field of interior building painting.

I hold a CAP Painting diploma and have worked on many types of projects: apartments, houses, offices, shops, co‑ownerships and professional spaces.

On April 1st, 2023, I created my company to offer professional, clean and transparent services based on solid experience and craftsmanship.

Areas of expertise

  • Interior painting: walls, ceilings, woodwork
  • Painting of doors and windows
  • Full surface preparation
  • Wallpaper installation
  • Glass fiber installation (toile de verre)
  • Apartment and house renovation
  • Co‑ownership work
  • Professional spaces (offices, shops…)
  • Personalized color and finish advice
